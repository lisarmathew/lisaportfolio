Clover, West Highland Terrier, 5 yrs.

Tips on how to properly care for your Westie

This is Clover, she is a fun-loving West Highland Terrier! She remains very energetic and curious. and is even good around small children. Arnold, Beagle, 2 years old

Informative Link on the Care of Beagles

This the cute and adorable Arnold! Arnold is a playful beagle who will love you forever. Furthermore, Arnold is well-trained due to his mature age, 2 years. Beagles are amiable, even-tempered, and great with children of all types. This is due to their moderate size and lively nature. Arnold is great for a walk in the park or even a trip to the beach. He loves turkey, rice, exercise, and you!

Kitty, Bichon, 1 year old

Informative Link on the Care of Bichons

Kitty is 1 year old and a bichon. Bichons are know for their elegant, charming, and cheerful nature. Being in her youth, Kitty yearns for the company of a family!
